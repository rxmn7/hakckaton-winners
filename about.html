Our Story

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing premium pet supplies and exceptional service to pet owners everywhere. Our passion for animals drives everything we do.

How It All Began

PetShop was founded in 2012 by animal lovers Sarah and Michael Johnson. What started as a small local pet store has grown into a trusted online destination for pet owners nationwide.

After struggling to find high-quality, safe products for their own pets, Sarah and Michael realized there was a need for a pet supply store that prioritized quality over everything else. They began sourcing products directly from trusted manufacturers and testing everything with their own pets first.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy pets and their owners across the country. While we've grown, our commitment to quality and customer service remains the same.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at PetShop

Pet First

Every decision we make starts with what's best for pets. We test products with real pets and only sell what we would give to our own animals.

Quality Guaranteed

We carefully vet every product and manufacturer. If we wouldn't buy it for our own pets, we won't sell it to you.

Trust & Transparency

We believe in honest recommendations and transparent pricing. Our pet experts are always available to help you make the best choices.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through eco-friendly packaging and sustainable product choices.

Community Focus

We support local animal shelters and rescue organizations through donations and adoption events.

Education

We provide resources and information to help pet owners make informed decisions about their pets' health and happiness.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the PetShop story

2012

PetShop Founded

Sarah and Michael open their first small pet store in downtown Seattle, focusing on premium products for local pet owners.

2014

Online Expansion

We launch our e-commerce website, allowing us to serve pet owners across the Pacific Northwest with our curated selection.

2016

National Shipping

PetShop expands to offer nationwide shipping, bringing our premium products to pet owners across the United States.

2018

Expert Advisory Board

We establish our Veterinary Advisory Board to ensure all products meet the highest standards of safety and quality.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

We launch our eco-friendly packaging initiative and commit to carbon-neutral shipping options.

2023

50,000 Happy Customers

We celebrate serving over 50,000 satisfied customers and their beloved pets across the country.
